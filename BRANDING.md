## Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice
- **Professional but Friendly** - Not stuffy, not overly casual
- **Empowering** - Help users feel in control of their time and money
- **Clear & Concise** - Freelancers are busy, respect their time
- **Data-Driven** - Back up claims with numbers and insights

### Writing Examples

**Good ‚úÖ**
- "You earned $247.50 this week. Nice work!"
- "Track your focus sessions and see exactly where your time goes."
- "3-day streak! You're on fire üî•"

**Bad ‚ùå**
- "Maximize synergistic productivity paradigms" (too corporate)
- "OMG you're literally the BEST freelancer ever!!!" (too casual)
- "Use our app to do stuff" (too vague)

### Messaging Pillars

1. **Transparency** - See exactly where your time and money go
2. **Simplicity** - One tool instead of three
3. **Earnings** - Focus on the money, not just the time
4. **Freelance-First** - Built for independent workers
